About the Club

We are a collective of curious minds at Mayo dedicated to making artificial intelligence understandable, accessible, and ethical for everyone.

Who We Are

The Mayo AI Literacy Club brings together students, faculty, clinicians, and community partners who are passionate about learning how AI is transforming health care, education, and society. Our interdisciplinary membership encourages collaborative exploration, whether you are new to AI or have years of experience.

What We Do

Through seminars, reading groups, and interactive demonstrations, we demystify complex topics and promote responsible innovation. We believe AI literacy is a team sport, and we provide the support and resources needed to grow together.
